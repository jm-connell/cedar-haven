Challenge Requirements:

  • Enjoy Stories

    Attend one book club & Read/listen to 12 mini stories

  • Enjoy Art

    Add featured art to your phone's wallpaper, TV screen or display a print in your home

    Attend at least three Cedar Haven activities

    Listen to the playlist at least 12x, about once a week

    Build a craft or make some art for each theme, attend a class or complete at home

  • Spend Time Outside

    12 hours, about one hour a week, feel free to break up into smaller trips
